Starting from the INFORMAL SENTENCE below, you are asked to rank 5 CANDIDATE SENTENCES (A,B,C,D, and E)
with respect to how good formal rewrites of the informal sentence are.
While doing so you should take into account:

  • the formality of the candidate sentence
  • the fluency of the candidate sentence and,
  • its similarity to the informal sentence.

The top ranking candidate should be the one that is the most formal, fluent and similar to the informal sentence in meaning.
